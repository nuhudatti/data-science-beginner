NITDA/NCAIR Legacy

For decades, NITDA and NCAIR have been the bedrock of technological advancement in our nation. Their unwavering commitment to innovation has paved the way for a digital revolution that continues to transform lives across every sector.

"Through visionary leadership and relentless pursuit of excellence, we have created ecosystems where innovation thrives and technology serves humanity."

ONDI Legacy

The ONDI initiative represents the future of digital literacy, empowering generations with the skills to navigate and shape the technological landscape. Its legacy is the countless minds it has ignited with the spark of knowledge and possibility.

"Education is the most powerful catalyst for change. Our mission is to light the path for those who will build tomorrow."

Milestone Timeline

2001

Establishment of NITDA with a mandate to develop Nigeria's IT sector

2010

National digital literacy initiatives launched across all states

2018

NCAIR established as Africa's first AI research center

2020

ONDI initiative launched to bridge the digital skills gap

2023

Legacy Learning Trail concept developed for eternal knowledge sharing
