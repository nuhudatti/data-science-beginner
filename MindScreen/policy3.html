Impact Assessment Models

Measuring Policy Effectiveness

Impact assessment models provide systematic approaches to evaluate policy effectiveness, efficiency, and equity. These models help policymakers understand the real-world consequences of their decisions and make data-informed adjustments.

  • Cost-Benefit Analysis: Quantifies economic impacts
  • Social Return on Investment (SROI): Measures social value created
  • Logic Models: Maps inputs to outcomes
  • Theory of Change: Illustrates pathways to impact
  • Randomized Control Trials: Gold standard for causal inference

Nigerian Success Story

The National Social Investment Program used impact assessment models to evaluate its school feeding initiative. By measuring nutritional outcomes, attendance rates, and academic performance, policymakers demonstrated a 27% increase in school attendance and optimized resource allocation.
