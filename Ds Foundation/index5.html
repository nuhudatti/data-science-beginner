Project Overview

Nigeria Economic Analysis Challenge

As a data scientist at the Nigerian National Economic Planning Commission, you've been tasked with analyzing the country's economic performance over the last decade. Your analysis will inform national policy decisions for the upcoming year.

Project Objectives

  • Analyze GDP growth trends across sectors and regions
  • Identify key drivers of economic performance
  • Predict next year's economic indicators
  • Provide actionable recommendations for policymakers
  • Create an interactive dashboard for ongoing analysis

GDP Data

Annual GDP by sector (2010-2023)

Source: National Bureau of Statistics

Demographic Data

Population, employment, education by state

Source: National Population Commission

Trade Data

Imports, exports, and trade balance

Source: Central Bank of Nigeria
